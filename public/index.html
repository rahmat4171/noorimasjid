About Us

Learn about our history, mission, and the team serving our community.

Our History & Mission

Established in 2025, Noori Jama-Masjid has been serving the Muslim community for over 10 years. What began as a small prayer space has grown into a vibrant community center serving thousands of families.

Our mission is to provide a welcoming space for worship, education, and community building while fostering understanding and cooperation with our neighbors of all faiths.
